# Annual Plumbing Maintenance Checklist for Christchurch Homeowners

Regular plumbing maintenance can prevent costly emergencies and extend the life of your plumbing system. Use this comprehensive checklist to keep your plumbing in top condition year-round.

## Monthly Checks

### Kitchen

**☑️ Check Under Sink**
- [ ] Look for water stains or dampness
- [ ] Check for mold or mildew
- [ ] Inspect pipe connections
- [ ] Test for loose fittings
- [ ] Check cabinet floor for water damage

**☑️ Inspect Taps**
- [ ] Check for drips when turned off
- [ ] Test hot and cold handles
- [ ] Look for corrosion around base
- [ ] Check water pressure
- [ ] Clean aerators if flow is reduced

**☑️ Dishwasher Check**
- [ ] Inspect hoses for cracks
- [ ] Check connections for leaks
- [ ] Clean filter
- [ ] Check door seal
- [ ] Run empty cycle with cleaner

### Bathrooms

**☑️ Check All Taps**
- [ ] Basin taps - hot and cold
- [ ] Shower taps/mixer
- [ ] Bath taps
- [ ] Look for drips or leaks
- [ ] Test pressure

**☑️ Toilet Inspection**
- [ ] Check for running water when not in use
- [ ] Listen for phantom flushing
- [ ] Inspect base for leaks
- [ ] Test flush mechanism
- [ ] Check water level in tank

**☑️ Shower Check**
- [ ] Remove and clean showerhead
- [ ] Check for leaks behind wall (if accessible)
- [ ] Test drainage speed
- [ ] Inspect grout and caulking
- [ ] Check for mold in corners

### Laundry

**☑️ Washing Machine**
- [ ] Inspect hoses for bulges or cracks
- [ ] Check connections
- [ ] Look for water under machine
- [ ] Clean lint filter
- [ ] Test shut-off valves

### General

**☑️ Water Meter Check**
- [ ] Turn off all water
- [ ] Check if meter is still moving
- [ ] Note the reading
- [ ] Check again in 1 hour
- [ ] If moving, you have a leak

## Quarterly Checks

### Hot Water System

**☑️ Visual Inspection**
- [ ] Check for leaks around cylinder
- [ ] Look for corrosion on fittings
- [ ] Inspect pressure relief valve
- [ ] Check temperature setting
- [ ] Listen for unusual noises

**☑️ Temperature Test**
- [ ] Run hot tap for 1 minute
- [ ] Check temperature (should be 55-60°C)
- [ ] Too hot = energy waste and scalding risk
- [ ] Too cold = bacteria growth risk

### External Taps

**☑️ Outdoor Taps**
- [ ] Test each outdoor tap
- [ ] Check for leaks
- [ ] Ensure they turn off completely
- [ ] Look for winter damage
- [ ] Test garden irrigation if present

### Drains

**☑️ Drain Flow Test**
- [ ] Fill sinks and watch drainage
- [ ] Should drain in under 30 seconds
- [ ] Listen for gurgling sounds
- [ ] Check for slow drainage
- [ ] Note any bad odors

---

## Bi-Annual Checks (Spring & Autumn)

### Gutters and Downpipes

**☑️ Gutter Clean**
- [ ] Remove leaves and debris
- [ ] Check for damage
- [ ] Ensure downpipes are clear
- [ ] Check connections
- [ ] Test water flow

### Hot Water Cylinder Service

**☑️ Professional Service**
- [ ] Pressure relief valve test
- [ ] Anode inspection (if due)
- [ ] Temperature pressure relief valve
- [ ] General condition assessment
- [ ] Efficiency check

**⚠️ Important:** Have a licensed plumber service your cylinder annually if it's gas, every 2 years for electric.

### Water Pressure

**☑️ Pressure Test**
- [ ] Test at multiple taps
- [ ] Should be 250-500 kPa
- [ ] Too high can damage fixtures
- [ ] Too low indicates problems
- [ ] Consider pressure limiting valve if too high

### Washing Machine Hoses

**☑️ Hose Inspection**
- [ ] Look for cracks or bulges
- [ ] Check for corrosion at ends
- [ ] Ensure connections are tight
- [ ] Consider replacement if >5 years old
- [ ] Upgrade to braided stainless steel hoses

## Annual Checks

### Professional Plumbing Inspection

**☑️ Hire a Licensed Plumber**
- [ ] Full system inspection
- [ ] Leak detection
- [ ] Pressure testing
- [ ] Water quality check
- [ ] Compliance check

**Why Professional Inspection Matters:**
- Catches hidden issues
- Maintains warranty
- Insurance compliance
- Peace of mind
- Prevents emergencies

### Hot Water Cylinder Anode

**☑️ Anode Replacement (Every 3-5 years)**
- [ ] Check anode condition
- [ ] Replace if corroded
- [ ] Professional job required
- [ ] Extends cylinder life significantly
- [ ] Cheap insurance for expensive cylinder

### TPR Valve Test

**☑️ Temperature Pressure Relief Valve**
- [ ] Lift and release test lever
- [ ] Water should flow then stop
- [ ] If continues dripping, needs replacement
- [ ] Critical safety component
- [ ] Legal requirement to function

### Drain Inspection

**☑️ CCTV Drain Inspection**
- [ ] Recommended every 2-3 years
- [ ] Identifies blockages early
- [ ] Spots root intrusion
- [ ] Reveals pipe damage
- [ ] Peace of mind

### Septic System (if applicable)

**☑️ Septic Tank Maintenance**
- [ ] Check sludge levels
- [ ] Pump if needed (every 3-5 years)
- [ ] Inspect drain field
- [ ] Check for odors
- [ ] Professional service required

### Backflow Prevention

**☑️ Backflow Device Test**
- [ ] Annual testing required by law
- [ ] Must be done by certified tester
- [ ] Ensure compliance
- [ ] Protect water supply
- [ ] Update council records

**☑️ Post-Winter Checks**
- [ ] Check for frost damage
- [ ] Test all outdoor taps
- [ ] Inspect exposed pipes
- [ ] Service hot water cylinder
- [ ] Clean gutters after winter

### Summer (December - February)

**☑️ High Usage Preparation**
- [ ] Check irrigation systems
- [ ] Test outdoor showers
- [ ] Inspect pool equipment (if applicable)
- [ ] Monitor water usage
- [ ] Service air conditioning drains

### Autumn (March - May)

**☑️ Winter Preparation**
- [ ] Clear leaves from gutters
- [ ] Insulate exposed pipes
- [ ] Drain outdoor taps
- [ ] Check pipe lagging
- [ ] Service heating systems

### Winter (June - August)

**☑️ Cold Weather Care**
- [ ] Monitor for frozen pipes
- [ ] Keep cabinet doors open (under sinks)
- [ ] Drip taps during extreme cold
- [ ] Check heating system
- [ ] Look for condensation issues

## Warning Signs to Watch For

### Immediate Action Required

**🚨 Call a Plumber Immediately If:**
- Burst pipes
- Major leaks
- No water
- Sewer backup
- Gas smell (hot water cylinder)
- Hot water cylinder leaking

### Schedule Service Soon

**⚠️ Book Within a Week:**
- Persistent dripping taps
- Running toilets
- Slow drains
- Discolored water
- Low water pressure
- Unusual pipe noises

### Monitor Closely

**👀 Keep an Eye On:**
- Slightly higher water bills
- Minor leaks
- Occasional drain slowdowns
- Old fixtures
- Aging hot water cylinder
