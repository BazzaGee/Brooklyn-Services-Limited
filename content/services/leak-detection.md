## Our Leak Detection Services

### Acoustic Leak Detection

Using sensitive listening equipment to detect the sound of escaping water.

**How Acoustic Leak Detection Works:**
- Specialized microphones amplify leak sounds
- Ground microphones for underground pipes
- Contact microphones for surface pipes
- Correlation equipment for precise location
- Noise filtering for accurate results

**What We Can Detect:**
- Underground water leaks
- Slab leaks
- Wall cavity leaks
- Pipe leaks in concrete
- Hidden plumbing leaks
- Pressure pipe leaks

**Advantages:**
- Non-destructive testing
- Works on various pipe materials
- Accurate location pinpointing
- Cost-effective
- No excavation required for detection

### Thermal Imaging Leak Detection

Using infrared cameras to visualize temperature differences caused by leaks.

**How Thermal Imaging Works:**
- Infrared camera detects temperature variations
- Leaking water creates thermal signatures
- Wet areas appear cooler or warmer
- Images reveal leak patterns
- Precise location identification

**What Thermal Imaging Reveals:**
- Moisture intrusion
- Water-damaged areas
- Plumbing leaks behind walls
- Roof leaks
- Underfloor heating leaks
- Insulation problems

**Applications:**
- Residential leak detection
- Commercial building surveys
- Insurance assessments
- Pre-purchase inspections
- Moisture mapping

## Signs You Need Leak Detection

### High Water Bills
- Bills increasing without explanation
- Usage higher than normal
- Sudden spikes in consumption

### Visible Signs
- Damp patches on walls or ceilings
- Water stains
- Peeling paint or wallpaper
- Soggy ground patches

### Sound Indicators
- Sound of running water when taps off
- Hissing sounds
- Gurgling in pipes
- Water hammer

### Other Signs
- Musty or moldy odors
- Foundation cracks
- Uneven floor surfaces
- Low water pressure
- Hot spots on floor (slab leaks)
- Water meter spinning
