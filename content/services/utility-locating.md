## Our Utility Locating Services

### GPR (Ground Penetrating Radar)

The gold standard in utility detection. GPR uses radar pulses to image the subsurface, revealing buried utilities without excavation.

**How GPR Works:**
- Electromagnetic radar pulses penetrate the ground
- Pulses reflect off buried objects
- Receiver captures reflected signals
- Software creates real-time images
- Technicians interpret results

**What GPR Can Detect:**
- Metal pipes and cables
- Plastic pipes (with tracing wire)
- Concrete structures
- Void spaces
- Changes in soil conditions
- Depth up to several meters

**Advantages of GPR:**
- Non-destructive testing
- No service shutdown required
- Real-time results
- Accurate depth measurement
- Large area coverage
- Works on various surfaces

### Electromagnetic Locating

The most common method for locating metallic utilities.

**How Electromagnetic Locating Works:**
- Transmitter induces signal onto metallic lines
- Receiver detects electromagnetic field
- Signal strength indicates location
- Depth estimation capabilities

**What Electromagnetic Locating Detects:**
- Electrical cables
- Metal water pipes
- Gas lines
- Communication cables
- Traceable non-metallic pipes

**Two Methods:**

1. **Active Locating**  
   - Transmitter connected to line
   - Signal applied directly
   - Highly accurate
   - Can distinguish multiple lines

2. **Passive Locating**  
   - Detects existing signals
   - No direct connection needed
   - Locates energized power lines
   - Radio frequency detection
