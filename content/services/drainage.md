## Signs You Need Drainage Services

### Blocked Drains
- Water draining slowly
- Gurgling sounds
- Bad odors
- Water backing up
- Multiple fixtures affected

### Drainage Issues
- Wet patches in yard
- Sinking ground
- Foundation dampness
- Sewer odors
- Overflowing gully traps

### Maintenance Needs
- It's been years since last inspection
- Buying a new property
- Planning renovations
- Trees near drain lines
- Older clay or concrete pipes
