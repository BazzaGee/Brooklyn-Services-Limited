## Before You Call

### For Faster Service, Please Have Ready:

- **Your address** and contact number
- **Description of the problem**
- **When the issue started**
- **Any steps you've already taken**
- **Photos** of the problem area (if possible)

### For Drainage Issues:

- Location of the blockage
- Whether multiple fixtures are affected
- Any unusual sounds or smells
- When the problem started

### For Leak Detection:

- Approximate location of suspected leak
- Whether water meter is running when all taps are off
- Any visible signs of water damage
- How long you've noticed the issue
