# About Brooklyn Services Limited

## Our Story

Brooklyn Services was founded by **Sam Aitken**, a Christchurch plumber and drainlayer who completed his apprenticeship before deciding to go out on his own to help the people of Canterbury. The company name "Brooklyn" was chosen in honor of Sam's son—a personal touch that reflects our family values.

### From Sole Trader to Industry Leader

**Started as:** Sam Aitken Plumbing (sole trader)

**Current Status:** Brooklyn Services Limited (NZ Limited Company, registered July 4, 2011)

**Growth Pattern:** We began with a small client base, contracting to local plumbing businesses, and have grown into a multi-service company while maintaining relationships with the businesses that helped us during our startup phase.

## Why We Started

Sam Aitken saw a need in Canterbury for a plumbing company that combined technical expertise with personalized customer care. By building a team of licensed professionals and investing in the latest technology—from GPR for utility locating to CCTV for drain inspection—we've positioned Brooklyn Services as Canterbury's go-to experts for all plumbing, drainage, and detection needs.
