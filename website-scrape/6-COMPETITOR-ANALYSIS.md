## COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE ANALYSIS

### Brooklyn Services' Unique Positioning

#### 1. **No After Hours Call Out Fee** ⭐ DIFFERENTIATOR
- **Industry Standard:** Most plumbers charge extra for after-hours
- **Brooklyn Advantage:** One of the few companies with NO extra fee
- **Marketing Angle:** "We prefer to keep your services running 24/7"

#### 2. **Multi-Service Integration**
- **Competitor Model:** Most specialize in one area
- **Brooklyn Model:** Plumbing + Drainage + Detection + Locating
- **Customer Benefit:** One call for multiple services
- **Convenience Factor:** "Organise everything in one phone call"

#### 3. **Authorized Service Agent Status**
- **Rinnai:** Canterbury-wide authorized service agent
- **Electrolux:** Service agents for Canterbury
- **Competitor Gap:** Not all competitors have manufacturer authorization
- **Trust Signal:** Official partnerships with major brands

#### 4. **EQC/Earthquake Specialization**
- **Canterbury Context:** Post-earthquake environment
- **Brooklyn Expertise:** CCTV reports for EQC claims
- **Market Need:** Ongoing earthquake-related drainage issues
- **Historical Note:** Claims still being accepted by Natural Hazards Commission

#### 5. **Technology Investment**
- **Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR)**
- **CCTV drain cameras with sonde**
- **Acoustic leak detection**
- **Ultra-sonic technology**
- **Competitor Gap:** Many smaller operators lack advanced equipment

#### 6. **South Island Coverage**
- **Local Base:** Christchurch
- **Extended Reach:** South Island-wide for locating/detection
- **Competitor Limitation:** Many competitors are local-only

#### 7. **Large Project Experience**
- **Client Portfolio:** Major infrastructure companies
- **Projects:** UFB rollout, roading, retirement villages
- **Capability:** Both domestic and large commercial
