## COMPANY ORIGIN & HISTORY

### Founding Story
Brooklyn Services was founded by **Sam Aitken**, a Christchurch plumber and drainlayer who completed a stable apprenticeship before deciding to go out on his own to help the people of Canterbury. The company name "Brooklyn" was chosen in honor of Sam's son.

### Evolution
- **Started as:** Sam Aitken Plumbing (sole trader)
- **Current Status:** Brooklyn Services Limited (NZ Limited Company)
- **Growth Pattern:** Started with a small client base, contracting to local plumbing businesses, and has grown into a multi-service company while maintaining relationships with the businesses that helped during startup

### Key Milestones
- **2011:** Company officially registered as Brooklyn Services Limited
- Started as sole trader with small client base
- Expanded from plumbing-only to include drainage, detection, and utility locating services
- Hired and trained apprentices to maintain quality standards
